## 🎯 How It Works

1. **Upload** — Drag & drop an image or paste a URL
2. **Analyze** — Our AI detects the scene category (Nature, Urban, People, etc.)
3. **Generate** — Receive a contextual caption with confidence score
4. **Explore** — Browse tags, alternative captions, and add to history
5. **Share** — Copy, speak, download, or share your caption
